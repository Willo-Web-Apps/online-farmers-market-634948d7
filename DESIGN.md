# Design DNA — Patchwork Market

## Visual Personality: organic-natural
## Layout: community
## Typography: elegant-serif

### Color Palette
- Primary (Forest Green): #2D5A2E → oklch(0.35 0.09 140)
- Accent (Warm Amber): #D4884A → oklch(0.66 0.12 52)
- Surface/Background (Warm Cream): #F7F3ED → oklch(0.97 0.012 80)
- Text (Dark Earth): #1C1C14 → oklch(0.15 0.018 90)
- Surface Mid (Parchment): #EDE8DF → oklch(0.93 0.018 80)

### Font Pairing
- Display: Fraunces — weight range 300–700 (warm, editorial serif with optical ink traps — evokes heirloom quality)
- Body: Nunito Sans — weight range 400–600 (friendly, legible, soft)

### Unique Design Choices
1. **Warm cream base throughout** — #F7F3ED never goes pure white; the warmth is constant
2. **Organic arc/wave section dividers** — SVG wave shapes between sections instead of hard geometric edges
3. **Editorial producer spotlight cards** — portrait-ratio cards with name, farm location, and story blurb, styled like a food magazine spread
4. **Hero with layered ambient gradients** — amber glow top-right + green glow bottom-left, grain texture overlay, mask fade to cream — evokes autumn market morning light
5. **Oversized Fraunces numerals** for impact stats — text-8xl in forest green, layered as background decoration behind stat copy
6. **No geometric grids** — sections use uneven two-column layouts, staggered card arrangements, and generous whitespace to feel hand-assembled
